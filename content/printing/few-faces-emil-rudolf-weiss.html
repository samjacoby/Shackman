A specimen book

I published this small specimen book in two editions, German and English. I was moved mostly by the Weiß Lapidar series--the same typeface on the titlepage below--but it includes standards like Weiß Antique as well. Weiß's display faces weren't given much of a name in English, _Series I & II initials_, but they're both classic faces that show up in fine book design to this day.
