Mark Twain

The Awful German Language

Learning German is hard. It is hard and it is not fun. Twain got that like no other. This essay, originally an appendix in The Tramps Abroad, details the trials and trevails of trying to master Duetsch, and was a great solace to me while attempting to do the same. Be forewarned: it is not funny if you haven’t tried to learn German. It is belly-achingly so if you have.
