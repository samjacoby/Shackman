A Few Faces of Emil Rudolf Weiß

A specimen book

I published this small specimen book in two editions, German and English. I was moved mostly by the Weiß Lapidar series—the same typeface on the titlepage below—but it includes standards like Weiß Antique as well.

Weiß’s display faces weren’t given much of a name in English, Series I & II initials, but they’re both classic faces that show up in fine book design to this day.
