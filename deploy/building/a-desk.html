A Desk That I Made

One week at summer’s end, I decided I needed a new desk. Or actually, any desk at all. No glue. No screws. One piece of 4 × 8′ birch-veneered plywood. The whole thing is kept nice and tense, mostly by the tight-angled nothces that keep the boards together—though the cables in the back don’t hurt.
